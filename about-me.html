More About Me

“From playing games on B&W Windows 95 PCs in Kindergarten to drawing tech concepts in my notebooks every day, everything has served as an epitome of future"

I grew up as a curious kid and loved technology. Since my adolescent years, technology has touched my life in one way or another. I always loved design and technology, and have learned about them in one way or another throughout my life. I finally decided to formally learn it and pursue it as my career in 2017 by getting admitted to DePaul University’s MS in Human Computer Interaction program.

I love to Collaborate and Innovate.

Over the years I have collaborated on numerous projects with friends, classmates and coworkers. In 2019, I collaborated with a couple of friends to create an art installation that converts your heartbeat into beautiful artwork! For our efforts, this project got recognized as one of the Most Innovative Projects in DePaul University!
