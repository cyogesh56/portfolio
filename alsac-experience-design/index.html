01. Identifying Product and Service Challenges

  Project 1: Donation Capture for St. Jude Call Centers

The Ask

"Ensure staff and volunteers can easily, efficiently, and swiftly process transactions over a phone call."

- STAKEHOLDERS

Problem

The current version of the app was created nearly 10 years ago without any research, ux intervention and feedback from the users. This has resulted in 10-15% of incorrect information being captured at every event, leading to extra hours spent by staff correcting them.

Screenshot illustrating ux issues in a website.

  Project 2: St. Jude Memphis Marathon Participant Check-in

Screenshot of St. Jude Memphis Marathon Participant Check-in application

The Ask

"Ensure employee volunteers can quickly and efficiently check-in marathon participants at event venue."

- StakeholderS & Team

Problem

Cash and check payments cannot be processed directly within the application. As a workaround, volunteers create dummy credit/debit card transactions with the corresponding value of cash/check payments. These dummy transactions are later adjusted once the cash and check payments are processed by the bank.

  Project 3: Brand Asset Management

The Ask

"Ensure everyone can quickly and easily add assets into their collections, perform bulk actions, add to events, edit and download them."

- Stakeholders & Team

Problem

Adding assets to collections or performing actions is currently a cumbersome process, and there is no option for bulk actions on multiple assets. Users have compared editing an asset to using the limited features and customization of Windows XP Paint version, highlighting its lack of functionality and flexibility.

A tool for managend distributing digital assets.

Project 4: St. Jude Donation Form

Screenshot of the St. Jude website donation page.

The Ask

"Ensure the Payment options are visually and behaviorally consistent across payment types and programs."

- Leadership

Problem

Payment options provided on the main donation form of St. Jude website appear and behave differently from the respective brand standards such as PayPal and Apple Pay. This causes confusion among users when they decided to choose their mode of donation.

03. Designing Human-Centered Experiences​

 Project 1: Donation Capture for St. Jude Call Centers

The apps depicted below are utilized by volunteers and call center employees who require quick, efficient, and accurate information input. Some key enhancements were:

  • The new design incorporates visual cues for script and eliminates manual tasks such as calculations, streamlining the overall user experience.

  • Quick and easy access to the last input and user information to redule cognitive load.

  • Improved visibility of system status at various locations such as when the screen is being recorded or which section is the agent working on, in a long form.

Illustrated screenshot of Donation Capture App

Illustrated screenshot of Donation Capture App

Slashed the error rates in each event from 15% to 1% and saved numerous hours needed to fix the data.

 Project 2: St. Jude Memphis Marathon Participant Check-in

The key aspect here was to enable new features such as check and cash processing and ability to undo actions. Also, all the statuses and important information about the participant was highlighted, so the user can understand what is happening just with a glance. Some key enhancements were:

  • Ability to process Check and Cash payments in the app, so users don’t have to use workarounds to check participants in to the marathon.

  • Eaiser visibility of important aspects of participant’s commitment such as commitment amount, goal completion status, profile status, and profile information.

  • Faster way of assigning incentives with improved workflow that takes less steps.

Illustrated image showing breakdown of zones in payment processing app

Illustrated image showing breakdown of zones in payment processing app

Eliminated the need to have workarounds in the application to check-in participants for the marathon.

Project 3: Brand Asset Management

A set of solutions were rolled out gradually included enhancement for findability, usability, usefulness, and to make the app future-ready. Some key enhancements were:

  • Improved findability of assets and pages via search overhaul.

  • Reduction in workload via introduction of bulk actions such as multi-select, select all, download all, among others.

  • Emphasis on various features so easy to understand and use that require minimal visual cues and no training.

Mockup of Brand Asset Management application in desktop and mobile

Increased user engagement and retention and reduced competing 3rd-party app usage.

 Project 4: St. Jude Donation Form

Desktop and Mobile prototype mockups of St. Jude donation form

With a host of ways to donate to St. Jude, the goal was to improve the visibility of relevant payment options, based on the user’s device, and improve the behavior of each payment type. Key enhancements were:

  • Converted black& blue buttons to the respective payment provider’s button styles for easier recognition.

  • Streamlined the behavior of each payment method using Doherthy Threshold for less wait times when making payments.

  • Utilized Hick’s Law to reduce the decision complexity on mobile donations for faster donation experience.

Increased the overall donations by 1% by improving the donation speed and cognitive load.
