CommunicationAssist UX Design

A cross-platform app UX to aid people with Motor Neuron disease in communicating with the world.

Challenge

Create a non-traditional interface and explore how familiar screen based heuristics can be extended into new modes of interaction and technology.

Domain and Theme

Statistics of Motor Neuron Disease

  • Every 2 people per 100,000 are diagnosed with ALS.

  • Usually leads to death within 3-5 years.

  • Less than 5% survive more than 10 years.

  • Can only be diagnosed clinically.

Problems with current assistive methods

  • Inability to be used by the patient himself.

  • Figures not always represent the mental model.

  • Cartoonish look.

  • Expensive.

  • Bad UX.

  • Time consuming

-Anonymous user

Usability Test Results

The good

  • 4 out of 5 participants said that the prototype is easy to use.

  • 4 out of 5 participants said they will use this prototype.

  • 5 out of 5 participants clicked on the correct category for first 2 scenarios.

Things to improve

  • More accessibility friendly.

  • Ability to create conversations using predefined messages.

  • Inability to express pain easily.

  • Testing with real patients.
